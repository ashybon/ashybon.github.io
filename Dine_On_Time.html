Dine on Time Physical & Digital Marketing

Dine On Time was a food ordering app for the restaurants in the mall so that you could order food ahead of time and skip the line or have the food delivered to you. I was one of three designers to create the digital and physical marketing for the app. This included facebook ads, BART ads, Signage throughout the mall and email marketing.
